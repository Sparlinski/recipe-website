CHICKEN THAI NOODLE

This Thai noodle curry is full of flavor and heat that is sure to please the senses, made from simple and healthy ingredients, with juicy marinated chicken and fresh crispy stir-fried vegetables. Pair with your choice of egg noodle for a delicious dish.

INGREDIENTSserves 5-6

  • FOR THE CHICKEN MARINADE
  • 2 pounds chicken
  • 2 Thai chilis
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 3 tablespoons minced lemon grass
  • 1/3 cup lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/2 tablespoon pepper
  • 3 tablespoons cococunt oil
  • FOR THE SAUCE
  • 14.5 ounce can fire roasted tomatoes
  • 14.5 ounce can coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon ginger
  • 1 tablespoon concentrated chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons red curry paste
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • FOR THE STIR FRY
  • 1 onion
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 4 carrots
  • 8 mushrooms
  • 1 cup water chestnuts
  • 1 cup chopped pinapple
  • 2 cups bean sprouts

DIRECTIONSprep - 30mins | cook - 20mins

  1. Start by marinading the chicken. Slice the chicken and place it into a mixing bowl. Dice the Thai chili and add to the bowl.
  2. Add the lime juice, garlic powder, lemon grass, salt, pepper, and coconut oil to the bowl. Mix the chicken thoroughly in the marinade and set aside while you prep the rest of the meal.
  3. Next, make the sauce in a second bowl add all the ingredients for the sauce, and set aside.
  4. Next, slice the onion, dice the garlic, julienne cut the carrots, chop the bell peppers, slice the mushrooms, and set aside with the chopped pineapple and bean sprouts.
  5. When all the ingredients are prepped, the next step is to fry the chicken. Heat coconut oil in a wok on medium-high heat and add half of the chicken so you do not overcrowd the wok. Fry the chicken breast until almost fully cooked. Set the batch aside on a plate, then repeat the process for the other batch of chicken.
  6. When the chicken is finished, add some more coconut oil to the wok and toss the onions in. Fry for about 3 minutes then add the carrots. Toss and fry for about 3 minutes then add the peppers, garlic, and mushrooms. Stir fry everything for about 5 minutes
  7. Add water to a pot for the noodles. Cook the noodles as required. The rest of the stir fry will take 10 minutes so synchronize the noodles with the stir fry appropriately.
  8. With 10 minutes left, add the chicken to the wok with the vegetables.
  9. Add the water chestnuts and pineapple and bring the sauce to a boil in the wok.
  10. Reduce the heat and let the sauce simmer. With 5 minutes left, add the bean sprouts.
  11. Finish simmering the sauce and then serve with the noodles and enjoy.
