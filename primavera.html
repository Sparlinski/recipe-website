CHICKEN PRIMAVERA

A clean and elegant take on a classic Italian recipe, made with simple and wholesome ingredients. A sprinkle of your favorite cheese over the top adds the finishing touch to this simple and healthy dish.

INGREDIENTSserves 5-6

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 pounds chicken breast
  • 1 onion
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 2 cups of chopped green beans
  • 1 zucchini
  • 1 squash
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 tablespoon thyme
  • 1 tablespoon oregano
  • 1 tablespoon basil
  • 1/2 tablespoon pepper
  • 1 tablespoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 tablespoon concentrated chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup white wine vinegar
  • 14.5 ounce can diced tomatoes
  • 14.5 ounce can tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste

DIRECTIONSprep - 20mins | cook - 35mins

  1. Add oil to a large pot and bring to medium heat.
  2. Chop onion and add to the pot.
  3. Dice garlic and add to the pot.
  4. Chop zucchini, squash, green beans, carrots, and add them to the pot, saute vegetables while you dice your chicken.
  5. Dice the chicken and add to the pot, increase heat to medium high and stir eveything together.
  6. Add tomato paste, diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, chicken broth, and white wine vinegar to the pot and stir.
  7. Add seasonings and stir, bring everything to a boil, reduce heat to medium-low, and cover the pot with a lid.
  8. Simmer primavera for 15 minutes.
  9. After 15 minutes remove the lid and stir again.
  10. Simmer for a further 15 mins.
  11. Remove lid, increase heat to medium, and let simmer for 10 mins stirring occasionally to reduce sauce.
