WHITE CHILI

This white chili chicken recipe delivers flavor and the right balance of heat. Packed with juicy, tender chicken thighs, beans, and peppers in a creamy coconut chili sauce, it is sure to delight.

INGREDIENTSserves 5-6

  • 2 pounds of boneless chicken thighs
  • 1 tablespoon of coconut oil
  • 1 onion
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 2 red chilli peppers
  • 2 jalapenos
  • 2 cups of sweetcorn
  • 14.5 ounce can of pinto beans
  • 3 tablespoons chili powder
  • 3 tablespoons cumin
  • 1 tablespoon oregano
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/2 tablspoon pepper
  • 3 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon concentrated chicken broth
  • 14.5 ounce can coconut milk

DIRECTIONSprep - 15mins | cook - 35mins

  1. Chop the onion. Add coconut oil to a pot on medium heat. Add the onion and let them soften.
  2. Dice the garlic, red chili peppers, and jalapenos, and chop the bell peppers. Add them all to the pot and sautee everything for 5-10 minutes
  3. Cut the chicken thighs into chunks, raise the heat level to medium-high, then add the chicken to the pot.
  4. Add the coconut milk, lime juice, and chicken broth and stir into the pot.
  5. Mix all the spices and seasonings in a bowl to make the chili seasoning. Add the chili seasoning to the pot and mix into the pot.
  6. Bring everything to a light boil, cover and reduce the heat to a medium-low heat. Let simmer for 15 minutes.
  7. After 15 minutes, stir the chili and set a timer for another 15 minutes
  8. After 15 minutes, add the sweetcorn to the pot, raise the temperature to medium, and leave the chili uncovered for 5 more minutes. Serve with rice and enjoy.
