BOLOGNESE

A simple and classic dish. All of the flavor with simple and clean ingridients. Enjoy with a choice of pasta and garlic bread or a side salad.

INGREDIENTSserves 5-6

  • 2lbs mince beef 90% lean
  • I tray of mushrooms
  • 4 carrots
  • 1 onion
  • 6 cloves garlic
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/2 tablespoon pepper
  • 1 tablespoon oregano
  • 1 tablespoon basil
  • 1 tablespoon rosemary
  • 1 tablespoon of concentrated beef broth
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 2 ounces of tomato paste
  • 14.5 ounce can of diced tomatoes
  • 14.5 ounce cant of tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2-3 ounces spagetti per person

DIRECTIONSPrep 20mins | Cook 30mins

  1. Dice onion, add olive oil to a large pot on medium heat then add onions when oil is hot.
  2. Dice garlic and carrots and add to the pot, sautee for 5 mins.
  3. Add mince to pot and increase heat to medium-high, fry mince until meat is brown.
  4. While the mince is browning, wash the mushrooms, remove the cores, and slice.
  5. When the mince is browned, add diced tomatoes, paste, and sauce to the pot. Stir mince, carrots, garlic, and onion into the sauce.
  6. Add seasonings, red wine vinegar, and broth to the pot and stir well.
  7. Add mushrooms to the pot and stir again, bring the pot to a boil then reduce heat to medium-low heat cover the pot with a lid, and simmer for 15 minutes
  8. After 15 mins, stir the bolognese, and return the lid to the pot.
  9. Let simmer for an additional 15 minutes then remove the lid and stir again.
  10. Increase heat to medium and leave the lid off the pot, simmer the bolognese for 10 minutes to reduce the sauce stirring occasionally.
  11. Serve with spaghetti or pasta of choice and garlic bread or a side salad.
