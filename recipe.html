TURKEY BURGERS WITH CARAMELIZED FENNEL CHIPOTLE MAYO AND SWEET POTATO MASH

INGREDIENTSserves 5-6

  • FOR THE SWEET POTATO MASH
  • 200g sweet potatoes (per person)
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • salt
  • milk
  • FOR THE CARAMELIZED FENNEL
  • 1 fennel bulb
  • 1 small onion
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • FOR THE PATTIES
  • 2lbs ground turkey 85% lean
  • 1 egg
  • 8-10 sweet peppers
  • 1 tablespoon oregano
  • 1 tablespoon basil
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/2 tablespoon pepper
  • FOR THE MAYO
  • 1 cup mayonaise
  • 2 teaspoons chipotle seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper

DIRECTIONS

  1. Prep sweet potatoes by peeling skins, add pot of water to stove, add salt and bring to boil.
  2. While water is coming to a boil, dice onion, and fennel bulb. Add oil to a pan and bring to a medium heat. Add diced fennel and onion, dice gloves of garlic and add to a pan, fry, stirring occasionally until caramelized.
  3. Add potatoes to boiling water, reduce heat and reduce heat to a simmer. Simmer for 25-30 mins.
  4. While fennel, onion and garlic is frying and potatoes are simmering, prep the mixture for the patties. Dice the sweet peppers, add all seasonings and all other ingredients to a bowl, mix with hands.
  5. Seperate mixture into cup size servings and form into patties.
  6. If the fennel mixture is caramelized, remove from heat and add to a bowl.
  7. Add 2 pans to the stove, add oil and bring to a medium heat.
  8. Add patties to the pans, fry each side for 10 minutes or until internal temp has reached 165 degrees farenheit.
  9. While patties are frying add ingredients for the chipotle mayo to the bowl you put the fennel onion and garlic. Mix well and put the mixture in the fridge until the patties are ready.
  10. When potatoes are ready, remove from heat, drain water and mash with butter, salt and a drop of milk.
  11. Serve patties with a dollop of mayo on side, mash and a choice of vegatable like garden peas.
