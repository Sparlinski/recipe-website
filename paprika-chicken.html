PAPRIKA BAKED CHICKEN

A healthy and delicious dish that is quick and easy to make. Full of flavor with a touch of heat, sometimes the simplest things are the tastiest.

INGREDIENTSserves 5-6

  • 2 pounds of chicken thighs
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 3 tablespoons of smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon of oregano
  • 1 tablespoon of red chili flakes
  • 1 tablespoon parsley
  • 1/2 tablespoon of salt
  • 1/2 tablespoon of pepper

DIRECTIONSprep - 15mins | cook - 35mins

  1. Set the oven to 425 degrees farenheit.
  2. Diced the chicken thighs and put the chicken into a roasting dish.
  3. In a small bowl, add the spices and seasonings and mix them together.
  4. Put a small pot on medium heat, and add the butter.
  5. When the butter is all but melted, add the mixed seasoning mix and stir into the butter to form a paste.
  6. Take the seasoning paste and add it to the chicken in the roasting dish.
  7. With your hands, mix the chicken and the seasoning together until the chicken is well and evenly coated.
  8. Add the chicken to the oven when it has arrived at its required temperature. Set a timer for 35 mins.
  9. When the timer is up, remove the chicken from the oven, mix the chicken with all the juices at the bottom of the dish, serve with mashed potatoes and vegetables and enjoy.
