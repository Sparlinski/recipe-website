CHICKEN CACCIATORE

This take on a classic Italian dish brings so much flavor and taste it would surprise you to know how little it took to get there and how simple it is in execution. Bringing all the best qualities of the original with a clean and healthy profile.

INGREDIENTSserves 5-6

  • 2 pounds chicken breast
  • 1 onion
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 1 zucchini
  • 1 squash
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 4 tablespoons capers
  • 1 tray of mushrooms
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/2 tablespoon pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of concentrated chicken broth
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1/4 cup white wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons basil

DIRECTIONSprep - 20mins | cook - 35mins

  1. Add oil to a large pot and bring to medium heat.
  2. Chop onion and add to the pot.
  3. Dice garlic and add to the pot.
  4. Chop zucchini, squash, bell peppers, and add them to the pot, saute vegetables while you dice your chicken.
  5. Dice the chicken and add to the pot, increase heat to medium high and stir eveything together.
  6. Add diced tomatoes, chicken broth, white wine vinegar, and capers to the pot and stir.
  7. Wash mushrooms, remove core and slice. Add them to the pot.
  8. Add seasonings and stir, bring everything to a boil, reduce heat to medium-low, and cover the pot with a lid.
  9. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  10. After 15 minutes remove the lid and stir again.
  11. Simmer for a further 15 mins. Remove from heat, stir and serve.
  12. Serve with your preferred pasta.
