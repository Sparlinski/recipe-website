SUN-DRIED FETA TURKEY MEATBALLS

The feta used in these meatballs is a perfect compliment to bring out the flavors and seasonings of these delicious turkey meatballs. It pairs well with a delicious red sauce and pasta and a side salad to round the meal out perfectly.

INGREDIENTSserves 5-6

  • FOR THE MEATBALLS
  • 2 pounds ground turkey 85% lean
  • 6 ounce feta
  • 4 ounce sundried tomatoes
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/2 tablespoon pepper
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoon oregano
  • 1 tablespoon basil
  • FOR THE RED SAUCE
  • 2 14.5 ounce can tomato sauce
  • 2 cloves crushed galic
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tablespoon oregano
  • 1 tablespoon basil
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 2 tablespoon red wine vinegar

DIRECTIONSprep - 30mins | cook - 35mins

  1. Pre-heat oven to 375deg.
  2. Dice the bell pepper, then add to a large bowl with all other ingredients for the meatballs. With your hands, mix the ingredients well.
  3. Form meatballs with your hand from the mixture, you can use a 1/4 measuring cup to portion each meatball or eye to about the size of a small plumb. Place each rolled meatball into an oven dish, the mixture makes about 15 meatballs
  4. Place the meatballs into the oven and set a timer for 35mins or until internal temp reaches 165deg
  5. While the meatballs are in the oven, start working on the sauce. Peel the garlic and crush, lightly fry the garlic in a pot until the garlic begins to liquefy.
  6. Add the tomato sauce, seasoning, and red wine vinegar to the pot. Stir well and bring the sauce just to a boil.
  7. Reduce the temperature to low, cover the sauce, and let simmer while the meatballs are cooking. Make sure to stir the sauce occasionally.
  8. Serve the meatballs on a bed of spaghetti or pasta with a helping of the red sauce.
