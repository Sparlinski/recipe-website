  • THAI CURRY TURKEY MEATBALLS

    This recipe is heavily inspired by Thai cuisine with a compliment of roasted sweet potatoes to pair with the delicious lemongrass gravy. Just the right amount of heat brings this dish together, with the sweetness of the potato pairing with the curry-infused turkey meatballs.

  • WHITE CHILI

    This chili separates itself from a traditional chili recipe by infusing a lighter taste profile but lacking none of the taste. Providing a delicious heat that combines the best elements of traditional chili and gives a different profile and substance.

  • CHICKEN MARSALA

    This chicken marsala takes the best elements of the traditional dish and augments it to give a healthier profile and removes some of the sugars typically used. By swapping out red wine vinegar for marsala wine you can reduce the sugar content that the marsala wine or sherry brings without sacrificing any of the flavors.

  • SWEET POTATO SHEPARDS PIE

    Shepherd's pie is a simple and heartwarming meal. This version has reduced the "extras" that you tend to find throughout the various approaches to this dish. It is a stripped-down clean version and still brings that heartwarming taste.

  • TURKEY BURGERS WITH CARAMELIZED FENNEL CHIPOTLE MAYO

    This recipe evolved by taking each of its elements and adjusting them to work in synergy with each other. The turkey burger flavor in enhanced by the creamy chipotle mayo and complimented perfectly by the sweetness of the potato mash.
