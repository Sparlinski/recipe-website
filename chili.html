CHILI

It's a classic chili recipe. Chilis come in all different styles, flavors, and heat levels, and this one is no different. No frills, just a classic, clean, sugar-free delicious chili that packs the right amount of heat.

INGREDIENTSserves 5-6

  • 1 pound minced beef 90% lean
  • 1 onion
  • 6 cloves garlic
  • 14.5 ounce can red kidney beans
  • 14.5 ounce can pinto beans
  • 3 tablespoon chili powder
  • 2 tablespoon cumin
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/2 tablespoon pepper
  • 1/2 tablespoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 tablespoon concentrated beef broth
  • 4 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 14.5 ounce can tomato sauce
  • 14.5 ounce can diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups sweetcorn
  • 1 dollop sour cream (serving)

DIRECTIONSprep - 20mins | cook - 1hr 20mins

  1. Add a large pot to medium heat with some olive oil.
  2. Dice onion and add to pot, fry for 5 mins.
  3. Dice garlic and add to the pot, fry for 5 mins more.
  4. Raise heat to medium high then add mince and fry until browned.
  5. While mince is frying, add all the spices, salt, and pepper to a bowl and mix thoroughly. When the mince is browned, add the tomato paste, sauce, and diced tomatoes to the pot. Stir well.
  6. When the sauce is mixed in, add the mixed spices to the pot, and stir thou rally again.
  7. When the sauce, meat, and spices are mixed, add the kidney and pinto beans to the pot, stir again, Bring everything to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and cover.
  8. Let the chili simmer at a low temperature for 30 mins.
  9. After 30 mins remove the lid and stir thourally, ensure to mix back in any of the sauce that may be congealing on the side of the pot. Cover again and let simmer for 15 more minutes.
  10. Next add the sweetcorn to the pot, raise heat to medium-low, cover and simmer for 10 more mins.
  11. After 10 mins, raise the heat to medium and remove the lid. Let the chili simmer like this for 10 more minutes, allowing any of the excess liquid to cook off.
  12. When the chili is ready, serve on a bed of rice and a dollop of sour cream on top of the chili, and enjoy!
