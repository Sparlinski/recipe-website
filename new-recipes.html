Baconlicious

BACON WRAPPED MEATBALLS

Everything is better wrapped in bacon! This delicious take on meatballs combines the elements of a traditional meatloaf recipe and adds a twist by wrapping each meatball in delicious bacon. It pairs well with the traditional mashed potatoes that meatloaf is known for.

Smokey

TURKEY OLIVE SMOKED PAPRIKA MEATBALLS

This creative combination of flavors really makes for a unique dish. The smokiness of the paprika combines with the saltiness of the olives to complement the acidity of the fire-roasted tomato sauce. Paired with pasta, you have a fine meal that is sure to win anyone over.
