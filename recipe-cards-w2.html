  • JUMBALAYA

    This New Orleans-inspired dish is packed full of flavor. The key to this dish is being familiar with the chorizo you use. Chorizo can be salty so it is wise to be cautious in the amount of salt you add. How well your chorizo holds together will also contribute to this dish's profile. I like a chorizo that holds together and adds extra substance to the dish.

  • POT ROAST

    A classic American dish combined with an Irish stew twist. This dish is made for a slow cooker dump-and-go approach, but it can also be done stew style if you don't have a chance during your day to start the dish. A full-flavoured filling meal with minimal fuss or effort.

  • CHICKEN CURRY

    This recipe has evolved through trial and error to deliver a delicious curry, removing the added sugar that some can bring without sacrificing any flavor. As with all curries, the type of powder you use is king, so don't be afraid to experiment to augment the flavor.

  • SUN-DRIED FETA TURKEY MEATBALLS

    The feta used in these meatballs is a perfect compliment to bring out the flavors and seasonings of these delicious turkey meatballs. It pairs well with a delicious red sauce and pasta and a side salad to round the meal out perfectly.

  • CHILI

    It's a classic chili recipe. Chilis come in all different styles, flavors, and heat levels, and this one is no different. No frills, just a classic, clean, sugar-free delicious chili that packs the right amount of heat.
