APPLE BACON CHICKEN HASH

At first glance, this dish has such minimal seasonings, you wouldnt be remiss to think that it lacks flavor. The minimal seasoning is more than enough to pair with the mixture of ingredients that coalesce into a grand experience of flavor, from the sweetness of the apples, the roasted nutty peacans, to the savory bacon, a fantastically tasty easy recipe.

INGREDIENTSserves 5-6

  • 2 pounds chicken breast
  • 6 slices of bacon
  • 2 large sweet potaotes
  • 1/2 lbs of brussel sprouts
  • 2 green apples
  • 1 cup peacans
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/2 tablespoon of pepper
  • 1/2 tablespoon of cayenne pepper

DIRECTIONSprep - 20mins | cook - 40mins

  1. First pre-heat the oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Place a large frying pan on medium-high heat with some olive oil. Chop the bacon and add to the frying pan. Fry the bacon until the fat begins to release.
  3. Dice the onion, and add to the frying pan with the bacon. Fry until the onions are soft and the bacon begins to crisp.
  4. While the bacon and onions are frying, roast the pecans. Place pecans on an oven pan and roast for 5-10 minutes until the desired level of roasted flavor is achieved. Remove from oven when done and set aside.
  5. When the onions and bacon are ready, empty them into an oven-roasting dish that has a lid. Next dice the chicken, and season with salt and pepper. Heat olive oil in the same pan used to fry the bacon and onions and add the chicken. Fry on one side for 5 minutes, flip, and fry for a further 3 mins. Toss chicken into the oven dish.
  6. Peel and chop the sweet potatoes, slice the brussel sprouts into halves, and add them to the oven along with the salt, pepper, cayenne, and roasted pecans.
  7. Cover the oven dish and put it in the oven. Roast for 20 mins covered.
  8. With 5 minutes left in the roasting time. Chop the green apples with the skin left on. When the 20 minutes is up, remove the dish from the oven. Remove the lid and add the apples. Stir everything and return the dish to the oven with the lid off, this ensures that the apples don't overcook and disintegrate in the oven. Roast for the remaining 10 minutes.
